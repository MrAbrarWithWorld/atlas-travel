Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

ATLAS is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information ATLAS collects, how it is used, and the choices available to you when using the service at getatlas.ca. By using ATLAS, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information Collected

ATLAS collects only what is necessary to provide a high-quality travel planning experience:

  • Account information — Name and email address provided when signing in with Google.
  • Travel queries and AI responses — Conversations stored to enable trip history and saved plans.
  • Saved trips and projects — Travel plans and project collections saved to your account.
  • Personal preferences — Optional settings such as passport type, home city, travel style, and custom notes. Stored locally in your browser and used only to personalize AI responses when provided.
  • Usage data — Number of plans generated per session, used solely for rate limiting and abuse prevention.
  • Payment information — Billing is handled entirely by Stripe. ATLAS never sees or stores card details.
  • Location data — Approximate city-level location, only if permission is granted, used solely to improve travel suggestions.
  • Device and browser data — Basic technical information used for service improvement and security.

2. How Information Is Used

  • To deliver personalized AI-powered travel planning
  • To authenticate accounts and manage subscription tiers
  • To save, organize, and retrieve trip plans and projects
  • To apply saved preferences to AI responses where relevant
  • To enforce usage limits and prevent misuse
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions via Stripe
  • To respond to support requests submitted to support@getatlas.ca
  • To improve ATLAS over time

3. Data Storage and Security

Account and trip data are stored securely in Supabase, a SOC 2 compliant cloud database platform. Personal preferences are stored in your browser's local storage and are not shared with third parties unless actively used in a conversation.

Conversation history is stored temporarily in local storage and cleared after 24 hours for guest users. Saved plans for logged-in users are retained until deleted or an account deletion is requested. Industry-standard TLS encryption is used for all data in transit and at rest.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

ATLAS uses essential cookies and browser local storage to:

  • Maintain login sessions
  • Remember preferences and appearance settings
  • Store cookie consent choice
  • Temporarily cache conversation history (cleared after 24 hours for guests)

ATLAS does not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking cookies, or sell data to advertisers.

5. Third-Party Services

ATLAS integrates with the following third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Google OAuth — Sign-in only. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe — Payment processing. Stripe Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — Database, authentication, and row-level security.
  • AI infrastructure providers — AI response generation. Travel queries are processed by third-party AI infrastructure providers under confidentiality agreements. Provider identities are not disclosed as part of our service architecture.
  • Google Maps — Interactive destination maps displayed within trip plans. Map usage is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Tavily — Real-time web search used to enrich travel responses with current visa and destination information. Travel queries may be processed by Tavily's search infrastructure.
  • YouTube — Travel video links are surfaced via the YouTube Data API. No personal data is shared with YouTube beyond standard API usage.
  • Vercel — Hosting and serverless infrastructure.
  • Zoho Mail — Transactional and customer support email system.

6. Data Sharing

Personal information is never sold, rented, or traded. Data is shared only with the third-party services listed above, and strictly to the extent required to operate ATLAS. Disclosure may occur if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users.

7. Data Retention

  • Guest users — Conversation data is stored in local storage and cleared after 24 hours.
  • Logged-in users — Saved trips and account data are retained until deleted or account deletion is requested.
  • Payment records — Retained as required by Stripe and applicable financial regulations.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data held about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your saved travel plans
  • Withdraw consent for optional data uses such as preference-based personalization

To exercise any of these rights, contact support@getatlas.ca. Requests will be responded to within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

ATLAS is not directed to children under 13 years of age. Personal information from children under 13 is not knowingly collected. If a child has provided personal data, please contact ATLAS and it will be deleted promptly.

10. International Users

ATLAS is operated from Canada. Users accessing ATLAS from outside Canada should be aware that data may be transferred to and processed in Canada or other countries where service providers operate. Use of ATLAS constitutes consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. For significant updates, logged-in users will be notified by email. Continued use of ATLAS after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
